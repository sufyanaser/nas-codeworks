# NAS CodeWorks — ICP, Services, and Commercial Rules

## Marketing ICP

The website should primarily speak to:

**Iraqi small and medium businesses whose daily operations have become too large to be managed efficiently through Excel, paper, WhatsApp, and manual follow-up.**

This protects NAS CodeWorks from being perceived as a warehouse-only or sector-only solution.

## Commercial ICP

The first commercial focus is:

**Small to medium Iraqi distribution companies and warehouses.**

Reason: this sector has a high concentration of operational pain:

- inventory
- orders
- sales
- reports
- Excel conflicts
- daily errors
- unclear follow-up

## Website decision

The website must not be written as if NAS CodeWorks only serves warehouses or distribution companies.

Distribution/warehouse examples may be used as practical examples, not as the full brand identity.

## Approved services

The first website version sells exactly three services.

### 1. تطبيقات سطح مكتب مخصصة للشركات

Starting price:

**starts from $500**

### 2. أنظمة أرشفة وإدارة ملفات داخلية

Starting price:

**starts from $900**

### 3. أتمتة التقارير وربط البيانات

Pricing rule:

Do not publish a fixed starting price.

Use wording that pricing is determined after understanding data sources and reporting scope.

## Forbidden service changes

Do not add:

- mobile apps
- websites
- AI services
- ERP
- CRM
- e-commerce
- general development services
- design services
- consultancy packages

## Pricing blockers

Before publishing final commercial material, resolve:

- Automation / Reporting price
- monthly support scope
- revision/change policy
- bug-fix policy
- payment structure
- currency and invoicing terms

Do not delay the website build for these unless publishing pricing content.
